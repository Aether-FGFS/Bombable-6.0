_________________________________________________________________________________________

   Notes: How to add Sounds into AI model.
_________________________________________________________________________________________


 
   -If we do not need to use other sounds for AI model, we can use our 
    bombable-weapons-sound.xml at relative path located under folder
    bombable-6.0/FGData/AI/Aircraft/Bombable-Sounds with relative path
    "AI/Aircraft/Bombable-Sounds" to add weapons sounds into model direct trough
    some-sound.xml located under fgdata/AI/Aircraft/model-name/Sounds folder. 


________________________________________________________________________________________



   -To have different sounds.. engine and other you must use sound under
    flightgear installation AI/Aircraft/Bombable-Sounds. Simlpy copy file
    bombable-weapons-sound.xml , rename it to ai-sound.xml and paste it 
    under AI/Aircraft/your model/Sounds folder. Add your sounds into ai-sound.xml
    do not forget to register new ai-sound.xml inside model.xml to play sounds
    and you are done. Bombable will play sounds from inside of your AI Sounds.
